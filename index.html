Shinken · 真剣

Focus, deliberately.

A quiet focus timer for one earnest hour at a time. Set a block, name what you're working on, come back to a history that tells you where your attention went.

iOS first. Free to try, one-time payment to unlock more.

01

Set a focus block

Pick a length — five minutes or three hours — and name what you're working on. A topic is optional, but it makes your history far more useful.

02

Stay with it

Put the phone aside. The countdown keeps running while the screen stays calm. Lock the phone if you want — Shinken honestly pauses, so you decide when to resume.

03

See where your time went

Every block you finish lands in History, grouped by topic and broken down by day, week, and month. Patterns tend to surface quickly.

真剣

Earnest focus, one block at a time.

Shinken (真剣) is the Japanese word for earnest, sincere focus. The app is built around one belief: a quiet hour, done deliberately, beats a noisy day.
